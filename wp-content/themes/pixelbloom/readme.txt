A clean, modern WordPress block theme designed for blogs and personal websites.

== Description ==

PixelBloom is a minimal block theme built to showcase your content with a clean, modern design. Perfect for bloggers and creators who want a simple yet elegant website. The theme is built with the WordPress Full Site Editor, allowing for complete customization directly through the WordPress interface.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in PixelBloom in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Editor to edit your full site.
5. Edit site settings, header, footer and content through the Full Site Editor.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I customize my site with this theme? =

PixelBloom uses the WordPress Full Site Editor. Just go to Appearance > Editor to customize your header, footer, and all other aspects of your site.

= How can I change colors and fonts? =

You can change colors and fonts through the Global Styles panel in the Full Site Editor. Click the styles icon (circle with paint brush) and you'll have options to customize the typography and colors.
